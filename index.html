About RSE Group

Accommodation Built for the Marlborough Seasonal Workforce

RSE Group provides purpose-built, fully managed accommodation at 36A Budge Street, Blenheim — designed specifically for Pacific Island seasonal workers brought to Marlborough under the MBIE Recognised Seasonal Employer scheme.

Our facility is MBIE-certified and RSE audit compliant, with five years of successful audits behind us. We handle everything: transport, linen, cleaning, security, and inductions — so employers and workers can focus on the work.
